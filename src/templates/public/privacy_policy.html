Privacy Policy

01

Overview

NWU Protection Services operates this platform to facilitate the safe return of lost items. We prioritize transparency and ensure that personal data is never exposed through the public visibility layer.

02

Information Collection

NWU Protection Services strictly collects information vital for the identification and categorization of lost items found on campus premises.

03

Data Security & Masking

Protection Services staff are trained to mask data before it enters the digital inventory. We ensure that only a visibility layer is public, preventing fraudulent claims while allowing users to identify their missing belongings.

04

User Access Roles

The system enforces strict role-based access control to protect data integrity and privacy.

Role Permission
Student / University Staff Read-only access. No login required to search.
Protection Services Staff Full administrative control, data entry and masking management.
05

Physical Verification

This system facilitate identification only, it does not process claims. Personal identity and ownership verification are conducted physically at the Protection Services. Data collected during the physical claim process is secured in the database and is not exposed on this platform.

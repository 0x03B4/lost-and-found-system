Terms of Service

01

System Usage

By accessing the NWU Lost and Found System, you agree to use the platform solely for identifying lost items. External users are granted read-only access

02

Accuracy of Information

While Protection Services strives for accuracy, the digital record serves as a general guide only. Descriptions are generic by design to maintain security. The physical item held at the Protection Services office is the final record.

03

Physical Collection Only

This system does not facilitate digital claims. All items must be collected in person at the respective campus locations:

Mahikeng

Building A16

Potchefstroom

Building F17

Vanderbijlpark

Building F11

04

Verification Rights

Protection Services reserves the right to refuse the release of any item if the claimant cannot provide sufficient proof of ownership. NWU is not liable for items that remain unclaimed or are disposed of according to university policy.

05

Role Limitations

Only authorized Protection Services staff are permitted to input, edit or delete item records. External users cannot claim lost items directly through this system.

How to claim your item

Follow these three steps to get your belongings back.

1

Check Online

Search the list to see if Protection Services has an item that looks like yours. We hide private details to keep things safe.

2

Visit the Office

If you find your item online, go to the specific campus building where it is stored. You cannot claim items through this website.

3

Verify in Person

Show proof that the item is yours to the security staff at the desk. Once they verify it, they will return it to you.
